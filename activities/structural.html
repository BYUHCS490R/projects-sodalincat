Header, Nav, Main, Footer

Header is the top part of the page. It has site name, logo, title and maybe navigation menu

Nav is navigation section.It can be links or menus to move around that website. It can go inside the header but don't have to be in header, also can links to other pages on the same site

Main is the middle/main part of the page. It has contain article, image and text

Footer is the bottom part of the page. It often has copyright info, contact details, or extra links.
