Cambodia Temples

Angkor Wat

Angkor Wat is the largest temple in Cambodia. It represents the heart of the Khmer Empire and is one of the wonders of the ancient world.

Banteay Srei

Banteay Srei is known for its beautiful pink sandstone and detailed carvings. It is small but very artistic and full of history.

Koh Ker

Koh Ker was once the capital city of the Khmer Empire. Its pyramid-style temple stands tall in the forest and shows the ancient power of Cambodia.

Bayon Temple

Bayon Temple is famous for its many smiling stone faces. It stands in the center of Angkor Thom and shows peace and spiritual strength.
