Images

JPEG is best for photos with many colors and gradients like real-life images.

GIF is best for simple images with few colors such as logos or animations.

PNG is unique because it supports transparency and lossless compression.

To resize a photo to the correct display size, you adjust its dimensions in photo editing software.

Resizing with editing software is better than CSS because it reduces the file size and saves bandwidth.
